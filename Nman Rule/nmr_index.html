About nMan Rule

nMan Rule is a one man band managed by Mark A Dixon of Batavia, IL. Original and Covers band and genre included are classic rock and blues.

Mark began playing guitar at an early age of 13 years old. He taught himself how to play with instructional books that family members bought for him.

He enjoys sharing his music with others and is always ready to play guitar. nMan Rule has been around for about 13 years and has a couple albums up for sale in different platforms:

  • SPOTIFY
  • BANDCAMP
  • CDBABY
  • FACEBOOK
  • APPLE MUSIC
  • REVERBNATION

We hope you enjoy listening to these digital albums and make a purchase.
