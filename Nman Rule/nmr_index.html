About nMan Rule

nMan Rule Rule is a Batavia Illinois based Blue/Rock artist also known as Mark Dixon.

nMan Rule takes his love of the sounds from Classic Rock and Blue songs and uses them as a foundation for his own recipes of original music.

Most recently, WFXF 103.9 in Crystal Lake IL chose to play ‘Frosty’ from ‘Born in a Bad Mood’ on their local music program ‘The Garage’ on December 23rd 2019.

Mark’s original music has been aired on four other local radio stations as well.

Mark plays a variety of guitars on nMan Rule recordings as well as providing Vocals, Harmonica and Synthesizers to create a full band sound which includes Drums, Bass, Strings and Horns.

The music is recorded in Mark’s personal home studio which gives nMan Rule the ability to control the recording schedule as well as maintaining complete creative control over the project.

Besides original material nMan Rule also performs a wide variety of covers including artists like Tom Petty, Eric Clapton, Buddy Guy, B.B. King, Neil Young, AC-DC and more.

He enjoys sharing his music with others and is always ready to play guitar and has his albums up for sale in different platforms:

  • BANDCAMP
  • CDBABY
  • FACEBOOK
  • APPLE MUSIC
  • REVERBNATION
